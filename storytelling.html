S ince the beginning of time, storytelling has brought people together through entertainment and education and Vivian Harries keeps this rich tradition alive. Vivian enchants children of all ages through her fun and delightful way of storytelling. From school-aged children to senior citizens, her stories not only entertain, but foster learning, as well. Her program contains several folk and fairy tales told with props and visual aids. She not only entertains her audiences, but also includes bits of history and asks questions to encourage her audience to think and learn.

Current storytelling titles include:

Lady Vivian
Get whisked away to enchanting lands through magical stories of folk and fairytales from all around the world.
The Good Witch of New Mexico
An enchanting character that shares age-old, spooky tales that have stood the test of time.
Vanna Elf
A jolly little creature that recreates holiday stories that reflect the fun and festive spirit of the holiday season.
Ms. New Mexico Cottontail
With the aid of a magical carrot, this character enchants children with whimsical and magical stories.

Vivian is continually creating new characters and welcomes the opportunity to work with you on creating custom stories for your special event
